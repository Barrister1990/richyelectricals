---

## ‚úÖ Link Building Best Practices

### 1. Quality Over Quantity
- Focus on high-quality, relevant links
- Better to have 10 great links than 100 poor ones
- Prioritize authoritative sites

### 2. Natural Anchor Text
- Use brand name: "Richy Electrical Services"
- Use variations: "Richy Electrical", "London electricians"
- Use descriptive text: "NICEIC certified electricians in London"
- Avoid over-optimization

### 3. Relevance
- Target electrical/home improvement sites
- Focus on local business directories
- Industry-specific directories
- Relevant resource pages

### 4. Diversity
- Mix of dofollow and nofollow
- Various anchor text variations
- Different types of sites
- Natural link profile

## üéØ Anchor Text Strategy

### Distribution:
- **Brand:** 40% (Richy Electrical Services, Richy Electrical)
- **Generic:** 30% (electrician, electrical services)
- **Location:** 20% (London electrician, electrician in London)
- **Service:** 10% (fuse board upgrade, rewiring)

### Examples:
- "Richy Electrical Services"
- "NICEIC certified electricians in London"
- "Professional electrical services"
